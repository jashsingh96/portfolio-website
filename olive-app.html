Goal - Understand how users categorize information to ensure a pleasant and intuitive IA for our users.

Amount of participants: 6

Outcome: User feedback provided us with valuable feedback and allowed us to refine our sitemap to be more intuitive and compact.

Usability testing

Goal - Identify user needs, expectations, behaviors, and prioritizes usability issues to generate actionable feedback for improving the design and user experience.

Amount of participants: 6

Outcome: Categorized errors within an adapted version of Jakob Nielsen's rating scale, using the information gathered in the report to solve pain points in the app.

Link to full analysis: Click here

Preference testing

Goal - Determine if a new color scheme was preferred over its previous iteration.

Amount of participants: 10

Outcome: 70% of the users preferred the new color scheme, resulting in a complete change of color in the Olive app.
