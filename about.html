ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Jash Singh, and I'm a UX designer that is starting out in the field. I'm passionate about creating beautiful user-centered designs that help businesses achieve their goals and improve the lives of their customers. My educational background is in Psychology and Chemistry. My professional experience lies in being an Office Assistant and Student Advocate in the Center for Student Cultural Diversity and Inclusion of my College, a caregiver, and currently an Area Manager at Amazon. My design philosophy centers around putting the user first. I believe that the best designs are those that are intuitive, accessible, and delightful to use. To achieve this, I use a range of tools and techniques, including Figma, Framer, User Centered Design, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, preference testing, user interviews, and card sorting to gather insights. One of my strengths as a professional is my ability to collaborate effectively with other stakeholders, having a broad spectrum of cultural awareness gained through living in 4 different countries. I understand the importance of clear communication and stakeholder buy-in when it comes to creating successful products and experiences, while also creating products that are accessible for all. When I'm not designing, you can find me going on a jog, hiking, spending time with horses or researching new topics to could learn. If you're interested in working together or have any questions, feel free to get in touch with me. I'm always excited to meet new people and take on new challenges. Thanks for stopping by!
